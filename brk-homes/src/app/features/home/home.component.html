Brand-New Apartments in Northlake, TX

Welcome to Jefferson Northlake, a brand-new luxury community bringing elevated apartment living to Northlake, TX. Choose from thoughtfully designed studio, 1-, 2-, and 3-bedroom floor plans featuring 10’ ceilings, wood-style flooring, oversized closets, in-home washer/dryer sets, and modern kitchens with stainless steel appliances, granite countertops, and designer finishes.

Private balconies, kitchen islands, and fenced backyards in select floor plans create the perfect space to relax and unwind. To enhance your luxury living experience, residents may rent a private garage and smart-home technology package which provides high-speed internet, keyless entry, and advanced access and security to your home.

Our community is built to enhance your lifestyle inside and out. Enjoy resort-style amenities like a sparkling pool with sundeck and cabanas, a cozy outdoor lounge with a fireplace and grill stations, and a 24-hour Matrix-equipped fitness center complete with an outdoor yoga area. We’re pet-friendly too with a fenced dog park designed for your furry companion.

Ideally located near top employers like Textron Inc., excellent schools, and local favorites like Babe’s Chicken Dinner House, Jefferson Northlake keeps you connected to everything that matters. Just minutes from the Texas Motor Speedway and Northwest Regional Airport, you’ll enjoy both convenience and excitement right at your doorstep. Schedule a tour today and discover your new home in Northlake’s premier apartment community.
